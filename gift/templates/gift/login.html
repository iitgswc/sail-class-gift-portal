Senior Class Gift

Senior Class gift is a popular practice which is present world-over (now gaining popularity among other IITs) which acts as a first step for the soon-to-be-alumni (final year students) in the Student-Alumni connect process. The Class gift will be an imprint/memory left behind by the graduating batch. For example, an art gallery. It would help to inculcate the culture of giving back to the institute (which was not present before).

Why Contribute

Many of us don't realize that mere government fund can never cover all of the IITG’s requirements. To support our innovative student projects, or to expand student life opportunities, philanthropy makes it possible. The portal not only improves transperency in the process but also gives you the freedom to choose your ‘wish’ and how much you want to donate for the same.

How to Login?

A first-time-user needs to enter his valid webmail ID and a password will be sent to it using which he/she can login into the portal thereafter. Upon logging into the portal, you have flexibility to choose how much you want to donate and can vote for your choice in the listed set of popular choices.

How to pay?

  • If you choose to ‘Donate Your Caution Money’, your entire caution money will be deducted (i.e INR 4000 for B.Techs and INR 2000 for M.Techs)
  • If you wish to donate amounts higher than ‘Caution Deposit’, then you will be re-directed to ‘Institute’s Donation Portal’ where you can donate as much as you wish to donate. In such a case, you are requested to fill the transaction ID in your profile in the ‘Class Gift Portal’.
  • Although there would be an option to decline both the methods and take back your money, we hope that our to-be-alumni will surely have their imprint in their alma mater as a well-wisher.

How to Vote?

You will be listed the set of popular choices and you can select one of the choices, vote for the same and pay through one of the payment modes listed above. If your ‘choice’ does not get sufficient contribution from the ‘Class of 2016’, your money belonging to this ‘bucket’ would be added to endowment fund and would be utilized for other purposes.
